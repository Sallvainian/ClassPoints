Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

What ClassPoints is

ClassPoints is a classroom behavior-points tracker for teachers, available as a web app and an iOS app. A teacher creates an account, sets up their classrooms, and records behavior points for students during class.

Information we collect

  • Account information: the email address and password you sign up with (passwords are stored only as secure hashes), and an optional display name.
  • Classroom data you enter: classroom names, student names, behavior definitions, point transactions, and seating-chart layouts. Student records are limited to the name a teacher chooses to enter and their points — the app has no fields for student contact details, photos, or other personal information, and students never sign in or use the app themselves.

There is no advertising, no analytics or tracking scripts, and no sale or sharing of data with third parties for marketing.

Where data lives

Data is stored in a Supabase database operated for ClassPoints, and is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS). Supabase acts as the hosting infrastructure; access is restricted per-account, and each teacher can only read and write their own classrooms.

How data is used

Solely to provide the app's functionality to you: signing in, syncing your classrooms across your devices in real time, and showing point histories and totals. Your email address is used only for authentication and account emails (password reset, email change).

Retention and deletion

Data is kept until you delete it. You can delete individual classrooms (removing their students and histories) at any time, and you can delete your entire account from Profile → Danger zone → Delete Account inside the app. Account deletion is immediate and permanent: it removes your account and every classroom, student, point history, seating chart, and setting it contains.
